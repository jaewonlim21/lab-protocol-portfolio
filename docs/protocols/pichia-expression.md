# Pichia Expression

## Overview
Expression workflow beginning from a single colony or frozen stock through BMGY growth and BMMY induction.

## Purpose
To generate secreted recombinant protein using methanol-inducible expression in *Pichia pastoris*.

## Typical workflow
1. Pick a fresh colony into small-volume starter culture.
2. Refresh into log-phase growth.
3. Scale into larger BMGY seed culture.
4. Transition to BMMY at target starting OD.
5. Feed methanol every 24 hours.
6. Harvest supernatant or pellet depending on downstream workflow.

## Representative parameters
- starter culture: 1.5–2 mL
- large seed: 25 mL or 150 mL depending on workflow scale
- induction temperature: 30°C
- shaker speed: ~235–250 rpm
- methanol feed: 0.5% v/v every 24 h

## Critical parameters
- inoculate from a clean single colony
- use actively growing cells for induction
- avoid over- or under-feeding methanol
- record OD values at defined time points

## Troubleshooting
### Low induction performance
Check whether the starting OD for induction was too low or too high.

### Variable results between runs
Confirm colony selection, seed refresh quality, and timing of methanol addition.

## Notes from experience
The expression page should emphasize not only the steps but the decision points: when to refresh, when to induce, and when to harvest.
