# Western Blot

## Overview
Transfer and immunodetection workflow used to confirm target identity after SDS-PAGE.

## Purpose
To verify target presence at the expected molecular weight and support interpretation of purification results.

## Workflow summary
1. Prepare transfer buffer.
2. Pre-wet PVDF membrane.
3. Assemble gel-membrane sandwich.
4. Run transfer.
5. Proceed to blocking and antibody detection.

## Critical parameters
- remove bubbles during sandwich assembly
- handle the gel quickly after electrophoresis
- keep membrane orientation consistent
- document expected molecular weight in advance

## Troubleshooting
### Weak signal
Review transfer quality, antibody conditions, and protein abundance.

### High background
Optimize washing and antibody dilution conditions.
