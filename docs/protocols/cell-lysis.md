# Cell Lysis Before Purification

## Overview
Protein extraction from frozen cell pellets prior to purification.

## Purpose
To release intracellular protein from pellet samples while preserving analyte quality for downstream purification.

## Representative workflow
1. Keep pellets on ice.
2. Calculate lysis reagent volume based on pellet mass.
3. Add lysis reagent and protease inhibitor.
4. Agitate for defined duration.
5. Centrifuge to clarify lysate.
6. Collect supernatant for purification.

## Critical parameters
- keep samples cold whenever possible
- calculate reagent volume by pellet mass
- clarify cloudy lysate before loading onto resin
- label every tube consistently

## Troubleshooting
### Cloudy supernatant after centrifugation
Repeat clarification spin before moving to purification.

### Low recovery
Check lysis efficiency, pellet resuspension quality, and inhibitor handling.
