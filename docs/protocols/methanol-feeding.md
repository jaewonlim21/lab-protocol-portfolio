# Methanol Feeding & OD600 Measurement

## Overview
24-hour post-inoculation monitoring step for maintaining induction and recording culture performance.

## Purpose
To sustain AOX1-driven induction and document growth during expression.

## Standard example
For a **25 mL** culture, add **125 µL methanol** to maintain **0.5% v/v**.

## Procedure
1. Prepare a clean workspace and sterile handling tools.
2. Gently resuspend the culture.
3. Open one flask at a time.
4. Add calculated methanol volume.
5. Remove a small culture aliquot for OD600 measurement.
6. Record OD600 and return the flask to the shaker.

## Critical parameters
- keep methanol away from flame
- open only one flask at a time
- log exact time of feeding
- keep the calculation method consistent

## Troubleshooting
### OD unexpectedly low
Review induction timing, methanol addition, and starting biomass.

### Contamination concern
Confirm sterile handling and avoid leaving flasks open between steps.
