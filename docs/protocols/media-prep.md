# Preparing BMGY/BMMY

## Overview
Preparation of yeast growth and induction media for *Pichia pastoris* workflows.

## Purpose
To generate consistent base media for pre-culture growth in BMGY and induction in BMMY.

## Key composition
- yeast extract: 10 g
- peptone: 20 g
- water: 700 mL before supplement addition
- 1 M potassium phosphate buffer (pH 6.0): 100 mL
- 10x YNB: 100 mL
- 500x biotin stock: 2 mL
- 10x glycerol for BMGY: 100 mL
- 10x methanol for BMMY: 100 mL

## Procedure
1. Dissolve yeast extract and peptone in water.
2. Autoclave on liquid cycle.
3. Cool to room temperature.
4. Add phosphate buffer, YNB, and biotin stock.
5. Add glycerol for BMGY or methanol for BMMY.
6. Store at 4°C.

## Critical parameters
- confirm pH before final use
- add heat-sensitive supplements after cooling
- clearly label BMGY vs BMMY

## Troubleshooting
### Precipitation or cloudiness after storage
Check stock preparation quality and confirm sterile handling.

### Growth inconsistency across flasks
Review media preparation date and whether the wrong carbon source was added.

## Notes from experience
Keep this page tightly standardized because media inconsistency can make later expression data hard to interpret.
